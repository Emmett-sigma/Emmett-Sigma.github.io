Sigma Male

I can be a leader,
But I may follow you,
I don't need validation,
I do however appreciate it,
I'm don't talk much,
But you can talk to me,
I need space and peace,
I'm pragmatic and expressive,
I'm everything and nothing,
I'm hated and loved,
I'd rather talk to one that can appreciate me,
even if I don't know them,
I change many times over,
but my core values remain,
I no longer do what others expect,
I've shaped my ego to be my pleasant friend,
I'm an introvert but will leave a door open,
I'm made different and you can be jealous or proud of that,
It shows me who you are,
I'm a Sigma male struggling with the dragon of adversity...

Source wiki du poème
